How to Use Advanced Functions
Swing function
Airflow direction
LED DISPLAY
Press Swing button
The horizontal louver will swing up and down 
automatically when pressing Swing button. 
Press again to make it stop.
If continue to press the SWING button, five
different airflow directions can be set. The
louver can be move at a certain range each
time you press the button. Press the button 
until the direction you prefer is reached.
